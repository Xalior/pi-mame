# Alice 32

![Alice 32 at power-on](images/alice32.jpg)

- **`make kernel MACHINE=alice32`** — TRS / Tandy
- **Year**: 1984
- **Manufacturer**: Matra & Hachette

## At power-on

**PARKED** — stops at MAME's known-problems box (BAD_DUMP charset.rom, imperfect graphics); box held across two grabs 15s apart. The capture above shows the observed stop; the machine is not offered until the park is lifted by a policy ruling.
