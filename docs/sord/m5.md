## At power-on

**PARKED** — MAME refuses to run cartridge-less ("Fatal error: No cartridge inserted. M5 cannot run.", serial-receipted); baking a BASIC cart is a pending policy ruling. The capture above shows the observed stop; the machine is not offered until the park is lifted by a policy ruling.
